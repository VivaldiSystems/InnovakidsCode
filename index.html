What is a Learning Management System?

LMS is the framework that handles all aspects of the learning process. An LMS is the infrastructure that delivers and manages instructional content, identifies and assesses individual and organizational learning or training goals, tracks the progress towards meeting those goals, and collects and presents data for supervising the learning process of the student as a whole. A Learning Management System delivers content but also handles registering for courses, course administration, skills gap analysis, tracking, and reporting.

Features

Integrated
Is An Online Shareable cloud based repository for all your lessons, lesson plans, tests, quizzes, pacing guides, grade books, student general information, and resources.

Collaboration
Is a Collaborative Work Environment allowing teachers in your department, school, district or world to share lessons, lesson plans, resources, etc. Imagine resources from teachers no longer being lost forever when they retire or even great lesson plans created in England yesterday being available to teachers in New York.

Resource Sharing
Collaboration is more than sharing resources in Innovakids. You and teachers around the world use technology to design and build great lessons using a tool that makes this process easy.

Rewards
Reward systems are built into the Innovakids System allowing students to earn school points for achieving mastery of content areas as well as progressing those students to more challenging tasks.

Secure
Control systems built in for assigning lessons to students only after they have achieved a mastery level you set.

Individualized
The Innovakids System is based on a paradigm of the teacher being the facilitator of Learning. The Teacher designs the goals and sets the students free to achieve those goals. The student progresses at their own pace, but that pace is monitored and motivated.

The student now becomes a journey man responsible for their own advancement.
